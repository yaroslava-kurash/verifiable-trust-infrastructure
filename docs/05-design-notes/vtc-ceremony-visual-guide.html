One pipeline, every ceremony.

Every community ceremony is an instance of one decision pipeline. Pick a ceremony, edit its routes, drop a sample scenario into the simulator, watch which route fires, then version the policy and roll it back — fail-forward. The vocabulary matches vtc-ceremony-rule-ir.md and examples/.
